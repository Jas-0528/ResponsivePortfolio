Frog Breeds... Cause Why Not

By: Jasmeen Sandhu (Beyoncè Beyoncè)

Have you ever looked at a frog butt?
Casue I swear they are so funny for no reason.Frog butts are what made me write this post... well that and my Bio 11 teacher. Anyways here are some cute frogs and some facts about them!

Golden Poison Dart Frog

  • Lenght/ Weight1.5 inches, 8.5 grams
  • ColorMetallic golden yellow, black markings on snout and toes
  • Live near Pacific Coast of Colombia Prefer: humid, tropical rain forests, near rivers, temperatures of 26 degrees
  • LikesEnjoy the lowland floor of the rainforest, laying in dense plants and foliage
  • Food of Choice insects, flies, ants, beetles, spiders, mites, caterpillars and maggots
  • Other FactsToxins in the skin that can paralyze or kill predators,One of the most poisonous frog in the world

Malagasy Rainbow Frog

  • Lenght1.4 inches
  • ColorBlack, white, red and green pattern
  • LiveSouth Madagascar Prefer: humid areas, river or swamp
  • LikesEnjoy burrowing in soft sand and dirt, hunt at night
  • Food of Choice Eat insects and other small invertebrates
  • Other FactsCan climb vertical walls, claws and adhesive disks in front limbs that can scale rock walls and borrow

Black Rain Frog

  • Lenght/ Weight2 inches, 12 grams
  • ColorBrown with granules
  • LiveSouthern Coast of Africa Prefer: high elevations, temperate forests, shrubby vegetation
  • LikesNo Idea
  • Food of Choice spiders, worms and ants
  • Other FactsCan dig burrows up to 6 inches deep, don't need open water, can enlarge body about 2 inches
